WELCOME to sparque studio!

Sparque Studio specializes in uniquely designed handcrafted organic terracotta jewelry made from clay( baked earth ). Our jewelry collection is rare and visually an eye catching experience. The jewelry is hand painted to give an additional appeal to match your clothing both formal and casual. The terracotta tradition is a proud heritage from ancient India and offers us an aesthetic and functional alternative to artificial jewellery.   Being totally eco-friendly, we can contribute our mite in preserving nature and can proudly say “we care" !

Besides jewelry, Sparque Studio also offers original Contemporary Art by talented upcoming artists from India, Handcrafted Handbags and Hand-embroided Kurtis.
All products are imports from India and are GREAT AS GIFTS too!
